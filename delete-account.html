Account Deletion

Developer: Yumsoft

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

This page explains how users of Yumsoft mobile games and applications can request deletion of their account and related data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion by contacting us by email.

Email: cs.yumsoft@gmail.com

When sending a request, please include the following information:

  • App name
  • Login method used, such as Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
  • User ID or email address associated with the account, if available

Data Deleted

When an account deletion request is verified, we will delete or anonymize account-related data associated with the user, including:

  • Account identifier
  • Authentication-related information
  • Cloud save or game progress data, if stored on our servers
  • Other account-related data linked to the user

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where required by law, for security, fraud prevention, payment verification, or legitimate business purposes. This may include purchase records, transaction history, or records required for legal compliance.

Such data will be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

Processing Time

We will process verified account deletion requests within a reasonable period, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
